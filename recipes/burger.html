Burger

Description

A burger (short for hamburger) is a popular fast food sandwich consisting of a cooked ground beef patty placed inside a sliced bun. It is typically topped with a variety of ingredients such as lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and cheese. Condiments like ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise are often added to enhance the flavor.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef patty
  • Bun
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Bacon

Step

  1. Prepare the Patty Mix ground beef with salt and pepper. Shape it into a round flat circle and press the center slightly to prevent it from puffing up while cooking.
  2. Cook the Patty Heat a pan or grill over medium-high heat. Cook the patty for 3–4 minutes on each side until fully cooked. Place a slice of cheese on top 1 minute before removing from heat so it melts nicely.
  3. Toast the Bun Slice the bun in half and toast it in the pan for 1–2 minutes until lightly golden brown.
  4. Prepare the Toppings Slice the tomato and onion. Wash the lettuce leaves. Get your condiments ready — ketchup, mayonnaise, and mustard.
  5. Assemble the Burger Layer from bottom to top:
    • Bottom bun
    • Sauce
    • Lettuce
    • Tomato
    • Patty + cheese
    • Onion
    • Pickles
    • More sauce (optional)
    • Top bun
