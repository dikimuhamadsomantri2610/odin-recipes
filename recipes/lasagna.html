Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish made with layers of flat pasta sheets, rich meat sauce (bolognese), creamy béchamel sauce, and melted cheese. It is oven-baked until golden and bubbly, resulting in a hearty and comforting meal loved around the world.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil

Step

  1. Make the Meat Sauce Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté onion and garlic until soft. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Pour in tomato sauce, season with salt and pepper, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  2. Make the Béchamel Sauce Melt butter in a saucepan, stir in flour and cook for 1 minute. Gradually add milk while stirring continuously until the sauce thickens. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook the Lasagna Sheets Boil lasagna sheets in salted water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna Layer in a baking dish from bottom to top:
    • Thin layer of meat sauce
    • Lasagna sheets
    • Meat sauce
    • Béchamel sauce
    • Mozzarella cheese
    • Repeat layers 2–3 times
    • Top with béchamel and parmesan
  5. Bake Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Bake for 35–40 minutes until the top is golden brown and bubbling. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
